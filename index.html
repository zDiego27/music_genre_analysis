Music Genre Recognition

What is it?

Authors: Seth Gibson, John Dunkley, Diego Zamora Music Genre Recognition is the task of trying to make an AI that can determine which genre a piece of music belongs to. From converting the audio segment into the Mel-Frequency Cepstrum, to sorting and extracting features, to concluding and finalizing the decision to categorize the audio, AI will be able to achieve human-level accuracy when categorizing audio into different genres.
